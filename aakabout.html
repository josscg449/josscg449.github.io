About the Author

Hello there, my name is Joss Gantley. I have been around pianos and keyboards since I was about 5 years old. So I have decided to make a review website for all things keys, All About Keys. Some of the equipment featured in the reviews section is stuff I actually own and use for recording and live shows. I am a member of rock band from Saskatchewan, Canada called Dukes Under Fire. With these reviews, I'm not going to go into all the technical specifications but I will tell you about my personal experiences and why I think a piano/keyboard is good or bad.

Thanks for stopping by! If you have any questions or comments you'd like to direct to me personally please feel free to contact by email at info@allaboutkeys.net.
